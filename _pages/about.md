Hello, I'm Floor Verhoeven (pronounced somewhat midway 'Flor' and that thing you walk on). I am a PhD student at the [Interactive Geometry Lab](https://igl.ethz.ch) at ETH Zurich, and I am supervised by [Prof. Dr. Olga Sorkine-Hornung](https://igl.ethz.ch/people/sorkine/index.php). I hold a BSc in Human Movement Sciences and a BSc in Computer Science, both from Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam. I have earned my MSc in Computer Science with a specialization in Visual Computing at ETH Zurich.

I am interested in geometry problems that are related to digital fabrication as well as interactive modeling and shape editing. Research into practical algorithms for architectural and fashion design especially interest me.

In my spare time I like to go fishing, preferably on one of the Dutch canals and once a year with the National Women's team somewhere in the world!
